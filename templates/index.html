How it works

Three categories of student data, one powerful prediction engine.

Academic Analysis

Grades, study time, past failures, and support systems are analyzed to detect early signs of academic disengagement.

Attendance Tracking

Absence patterns reveal hidden disengagement. High absenteeism is a strong early indicator of dropout risk.

Behavioral Factors

Extra-curricular participation, social habits, and health indicators complete the student risk profile.

Random Forest ML

200 decision trees work together for robust, accurate predictions even with imperfect school data.

SHAP Explainability

Every prediction is transparent. Teachers see exactly which factors drive the risk, not just a score.

Interactive Dashboard

Plotly-powered charts: heatmaps, box plots, bubble charts. Real-time analysis at your fingertips.
